Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, the University of Melbourne (UoM). I received my master's degree in Data Science from the University of Melbourne and a master's degree in Software Engineering from Beihang University. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shandong Normal University. With over three years of experimence working at Microsoft MSN and Baidu, I am highly capable of data analysis and digital marketing. My research focuses on causal reasoning for dynamic models described by Ordinary/Stochastic Differential Equations.
